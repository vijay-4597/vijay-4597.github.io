Composites (Using KEVLAR)

This workshop is to train students on the basic fabrication of a KEVLAR fiber laminate by hand layup, cured by compression molding, and to introduce to them the procedures involved in testing the laminates for tensile, compression and flexural tests.
The students will also be introduced to impact strength determination and dynamic mechanical analysis.

Schedule of the Workshop:

  • Introductory lecture
  • Fabrication of laminates by hand layup
  • Testing of laminates on Universal Testing Machine
  • Laboratory tour to introduce Impact testing and dynamic mechanical analysis
  • Lecture on future trends in composites for the aerospace industry

NASTRAN

FLIGHT'18 brings you the technologies in the field of FINITE ELEMENT ANAYSIS and presenting them hands on experience with NASTRAN & PATRON software packages.
Participants of this workshop will be experienced with all the topics listed below,

  • Take a model through the complete design and analysis cycle
  • Import CAD geometry and use it to create new geometry in Patran
  • Learn graphics manipulation, imaging, and viewing techniques
  • Practice using the different meshing techniques: isomesher, paver, and tetmesher
  • Apply loads and boundary conditions
  • Submit analysis to MSC/Nastan
  • View and manipulate results in MSC/Patran
  • Hands on exercise
