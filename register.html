DRONE RACING CHAMPIONSHIP

  • Registration Fee:  ₹2500/- (exclusive of Taxes)
  • Age Limit: 16 to 24
  • Maximum 3 per team is permitted.
  • Participants will be asked to operate their drones in the given race course.
  • There will be 2 rounds and the winner will be selected on the basis of stability and time period of the drone to remain air-borne.
  • Download the document to know the drone specifications permitted to participate in the event.
  • All the registered participants will get the complete pack of rules and regulations for the event via MAIL.
  • Individual ID proof submission is mandatory for all participants.

*Online registration and payment of entry fee is mandatory for this event. For more info contact us
